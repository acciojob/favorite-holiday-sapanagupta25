Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its rich history, stunning architecture, world-class museums, and exquisite cuisine. Often called the "City of Light," Paris attracts millions of tourists every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The most iconic landmark of Paris offering breathtaking views.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to famous artworks like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe - A historic avenue filled with shops, cafes, and monuments.
  5. Sainte-Chapelle - A stunning chapel known for its magnificent stained glass windows.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant - A buttery and flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Ratatouille - A delicious vegetable stew from French cuisine.
  • Escargots - A classic French delicacy made of cooked snails.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes that can be enjoyed sweet or savory.
  • Macarons - Colorful, delicate, and flavorful French cookies.
